# Capture Points

Obtain:
    Capture an opponent's piece to gain points based
    on its value and your multiplier

    Queen   :   9 points
    Rook    :   5 points
    Bishop  :   3 points
    Knights :   3 points
    Pawns   :   1 point

Multiplier:
    Each capturing piece has a multiplier that starts at 1
    and is increased by 1 for every piece that it captures
